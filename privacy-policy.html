Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to FitAI Coach. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, mobile application, and services (collectively, the "Service").

By using FitAI Coach, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to transparency about our data practices. This policy provides detailed information about what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it to provide you with the best fitness coaching experience.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an account (name, email address, password)
  • Complete your fitness profile (age, gender, height, weight)
  • Set fitness goals and preferences
  • Subscribe to premium services (payment information)
  • Contact our support team
  • Participate in surveys or promotions

2.2 Fitness and Health Data

To provide personalized fitness coaching, we collect:

  • Physical Metrics: Height, weight, body measurements, BMI
  • Fitness Level: Current fitness level, exercise history
  • Goals: Weight loss, muscle building, endurance, etc.
  • Activity Data: Workout completion, exercise preferences
  • Progress Data: Weight changes, measurements, workout logs
  • Dietary Preferences: Halal, vegetarian, vegan, allergies
  • Photos: Progress photos you upload (optional)

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our Service, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent
  • IP Address: For security and analytics purposes
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session data, preferences
  • Location Data: General location (country/city level) for localized content

2.4 Information from Third Parties

If you sign up using Google authentication, we receive:

  • Your name and email address from your Google account
  • Profile picture (if you choose to share it)
  • Google account ID for authentication purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Purpose Description
Service Delivery Generate personalized workout plans, meal suggestions, and AI coaching responses
Account Management Create and manage your account, authenticate users, process subscriptions
Progress Tracking Monitor your fitness journey, show progress charts, celebrate milestones
Communication Send workout reminders, motivational messages, service updates
Improvement Analyze usage patterns to improve our AI algorithms and features
Security Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
Legal Compliance Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms and Conditions

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored using Firebase services (Google Cloud Platform), which provides:

  • Industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure data centers with physical security measures
  • Regular security audits and compliance certifications
  • Automatic backups and disaster recovery

4.2 Security Measures

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Authentication: Secure password hashing and Firebase Authentication
  • Access Control: Strict database security rules limiting data access
  • Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for suspicious activities
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and updates applied promptly

4.3 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide you with our services
  • Required by law or for legitimate business purposes
  • You can request deletion at any time (see Section 8)

Data Security Notice: While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but continuously work to protect your data.
