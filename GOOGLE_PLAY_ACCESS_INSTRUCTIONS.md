## How to Access Restricted/Premium Features

### 1. Launch the App
- Open FitAI Coach from the app drawer
- The app will show splash screen and onboarding

### 2. Complete Onboarding (if first launch)
- Tap "Get Started"
- Answer the fitness assessment questions
- Proceed through all onboarding screens

### 3. Access Paywall/Subscription Screen
The paywall appears automatically:
- After completing onboarding (first time users)
- When tapping premium features in the app
- Via "Upgrade to Pro" buttons throughout the UI

### 4. Test Purchase Flow
On the paywall screen:
1. Select any subscription plan (Monthly/Yearly/Lifetime)
2. Tap the purchase button
3. Google Play billing sheet appears
4. Use test card: "Always approves"
5. Complete purchase

### 5. Access Premium Features
After successful purchase:
- Full access to AI workout plans
- Nutrition guidance features unlock
- 24/7 AI coaching support becomes available
- All premium content is accessible

## Troubleshooting

### If Paywall Doesn't Appear:
1. Clear app data in Android Settings
2. Reopen app
3. Complete onboarding again

### If Purchase Fails:
1. Ensure device has Google Play Services
2. Check internet connection
3. Verify test account is signed in to Google Play

### Accessing Premium Without Purchase:
Premium features are locked behind the paywall. A valid subscription (test or real) is required to access:
- Personalized AI workout generation
- Nutrition meal plans
- Advanced AI coaching features
